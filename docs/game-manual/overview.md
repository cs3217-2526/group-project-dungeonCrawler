# Game Manual

> **Work in progress.** This section will cover gameplay mechanics, controls, and tips for playing Dungeon Crawler.


### Drop Button

Once clicked, the secondary weapon will be dropped on the ground and can be picked up again.

If the player has no secondary weapon, the clicking drop button will silently do nothing.

### Pick Up Button

Once clicked, the player will pick up the nearest weapon on the ground. If there are no weapons on the ground, the clicking pick up button will silently do nothing.

If picked up, the weapon will be the secondary weapon. If the player already has a secondary weapon, the old secondary weapon will be dropped on the ground.

### Switch Button
Once clicked, the player will swap their primary and secondary weapons. If the player has no secondary weapon, the clicking switch button will silently do nothing.
